Cheechaw's Rafting Adventures

We are a family-owned business that has been providing unforgettable rafting experiences for over 20 years. Our mission is to offer safe, fun, and environmentally responsible rafting trips for adventurers of all ages. While you enjoy the thrill of navigating rapids, you'll also have the opportunity to take in the stunning natural beauty of the rivers and surrounding landscapes. Our purpose is to create memorable rafting experiences that connect people with nature and each other. We strive to provide exceptional customer service, prioritize safety, and promote environmental stewardship in all our operations.
